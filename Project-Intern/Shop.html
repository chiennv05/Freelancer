How to use it?

With just 4 simple steps: put your shoes in a laundry bag, choose a gentle wash cycle, wash at low temperature and dry naturally, your shoes will always be clean and durable.

01

Put the shoes in the bag and lock it.

Simply place your shoes in a protective bag. Add a little detergent inside and outside of the bag. Zip it up and throw it in the washing machine.

02

Set and select the appropriate wash cycle

Add detergent (liquid or tablet) and select "low temperature" or "gentle" for the wash cycle.

03

Let the washing machine do the work for you!

Now you can happily enjoy a cup of coffee or read a few books while waiting for your clean shoes when the washing is finished.

04

Dry Shoes

You can either air dry your shoes or shove the laundry shoe bags (with the shoes inside) in the dryer - yes, it's perfectly fine! Or you can use our shoe dryer.
